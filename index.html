Dear Seafarers!

Welcome, we are glad to introduce you to our crewing company, based in Izmail,Odessa region, Ukraine.

You can trust us, as we always do our business in honest, professional way to satisfy all parties involved.

You can trust us, that we pay high attention to skill, qualification and high working standards of the professionals we recruit.

Our crew manning agency supplies our Clients with well-experienced and highly qualified marine specialists from single officer to full crew for Container Carriers fm 300 up to 9500 TEUs, Bulk Carriers (Panamax, Cape Size, Live Stock etc.) , Coasters, Reefer, RoRo/PCC, Chemical Tankers and others, and we will be happy to expand our business having enough potential and facilities for that.

Any reading skillful, capable seaman with interest of developing a career in a warm, friendly and healthy environment should not hesitate to join our company.

We can take care of crew change formalities throws our partners worldwide.
